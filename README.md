# Trading-Strategies
This repository contains an Excel workbook modelling multiple trading strategies using two years of MRF stock price and trading data. It includes implementations and analysis of trend-based, volume-based, momentum, volatility, and support/resistance indicators.
# Technical Indicators (Excel Models)
Real market data has been used.  
Each model demonstrates the mechanics, formulae and Decision of indicators used in momentum, trend and volume-based trading strategies.

---

## Trend-based Indicators
Identify the direction and strength of a trend.

**EMA (Exponential Moving Average):** Smooths price data to capture market trends more effectively than a simple average.  
**MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence):** Measures momentum through convergence and divergence of moving averages.  
**Heiken Ashi Candles:** Modified candlestick chart to highlight trend continuation and reversals more clearly.

---

## Momentum Oscillators
Indicators that measure the speed or strength of price movements.

**RSI (Relative Strength Index):** Evaluates overbought or oversold market conditions.  
**ROC (Rate of Change):** Measures percentage change in price over a specific time period.  
**Stochastic Oscillator:** Compares a securityâ€™s closing price to its price range over time to identify momentum shifts.

---

## Volatility Indicators
Indicators that gauge price variability and market risk.

**Bollinger Bands:** Uses standard deviations above and below a moving average to show volatility.  
**ATR (Average True Range):** Quantifies market volatility by analyzing the range of price movement.

---

## Volume-Based Indicators
Indicators that combine price and volume data to confirm strength or weakness of price trends.

**VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price):** Reflects the average price weighted by traded volume, useful for institutional benchmarking.  
**OBV (On-Balance Volume):** Cumulative total of volume adjusted for price direction to confirm trend strength.  
**MFL / MFI (Money Flow Line / Money Flow Index):** Momentum indicators that incorporate both price and volume to identify overbought or oversold signals.  
**CMF (Chaikin Money Flow):** Combines price and volume to indicate buying or selling pressure.

---

## Pattern & Price Action Strategies
**Inside Bar Strategy:** Identifies periods of market consolidation before potential breakouts.
